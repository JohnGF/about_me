About Me

Connecting domains, powering discovery.
I am a dedicated, systems-level professional driven by a passion for connecting knowledge across different domains. My background in Informatics, Meteorology, and Geophysics has equipped me with a unique interdisciplinary perspective, allowing me to approach complex problems with a blend of scientific rigor and engineering pragmatism.
Current Research: Currently, I am a PhD researcher in Computer Science, where I am designing a novel EEG brain device. This project is a testament to my expertise in AI, physical simulations, and data analysis, requiring me to manage a full-stack engineering process from sensor design to complex signal processing and data interpretation. This work, together with projects such as a patented tactile braille display, reflects my ongoing commitment to applying ideas practically and contributing useful solutions.
