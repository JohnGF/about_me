About Me

I am a dedicated, systems-level professional driven by a passion for connecting knowledge across different domains. My background in Informatics, Meteorology, and Geophysics has equipped me with a unique interdisciplinary perspective, allowing me to approach complex problems with a blend of scientific rigor and engineering pragmatism.

Currently, I am a PhD researcher in Computer Science, where I am designing a novel EEG brain device. This project is a testament to my expertise in AI, physical simulations, and data analysis, requiring me to manage a full-stack engineering process from sensor design to complex signal processing and data interpretation. This work, along with my history of innovative projects, including a patented tactile braille display, demonstrates my ability to transform groundbreaking ideas into tangible, impactful solutions.

Projects

AI, Data & Simulation

Novel EEG Brain Device with AI

My PhD research project, focusing on the creation of an advanced EEG/fNIR brain device. This work involves extensive signal processing, machine learning (ML), and AI to interpret complex neural data and develop a robust, functional prototype.

Dates: Ongoing

Shallow Water Python Toolkit with Floyd-Warshall Algorithm

A computational project that uses the Floyd-Warshall algorithm to calculate optimal paths in a shallow water environment. This demonstrates expertise in algorithmic design, data analysis, and physical simulations using Python.

Dates: 2024

Multidisciplinary & Systems

Braille Anywhere (Patented)

Inventor of a patented tactile braille reading device. This project combines electronics, embedded systems, and human-computer interaction to create a groundbreaking portable solution for the visually impaired.

Status: Patent in progress

Cloud Multi-Variable Meteorological Station

An IoT project focused on the design and construction of a low-cost, cloud-connected meteorological station. This involved hardware fabrication, sensor integration, and cloud deployment for real-time data analysis.

Dates: 2023
